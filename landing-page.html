Features

Everything Your Coaches Need

📚

250-Drill Library

Full drill library covering Technical, Tactical, Defending, Attacking, Goalkeeping, Physical, Set Pieces and The Streets Meet Tiki-Taka. Every drill has full coaching points, progressions and age group guidance from U8 to Adults.

📋

Session Planner

Build complete sessions using the 5-phase Coach Pro Method. Add drills from the library, set phase dividers, add notes, see total time, and save up to 20 sessions directly on your phone.

⚽

Tactical Board

Full-pitch tactical board with drag-and-drop players, arrow drawing tools, colour picker, formation selector, and opposition toggle. Draw passes, runs and zones. Export or share with your coaching team.

🎓

Coach Pro Method

A complete session design philosophy built into the app. The 5-phase structure guides coaches through Warm Up, Passing Activation, Technical, Tactical and Game phases with example sessions and timing.

👤

Squad Management

Track your players, positions, attendance and development notes. Keep everything in one place for every age group your club runs.

📷

Your Club Branding

Every app is built specifically for your club with your crest, your colours and your club name. Coaches open your club’s app, not a generic one.
